Summary

Xiao Zhan, an omega, became an orphan at the age of twenty. Their distant relatives, the Kyomotos, took him in as their adoptive son. He grew up with Kyomoto Taiga, an omega three years younger than him. Despite the stark difference in personalities, the two grew up close to each other and treated each other like real siblings.

One night, during dinner, Kyomoto Hiroki informs them about a potential marriage match for Xiao Zhan because of the need to keep the Xiaos company within the family.

Xiao Zhan finds himself getting to know his potential mate, Wang Yibo, before their families finalize anything. Feeling protective of him, Taiga comes with. Wang Yibo, not one to back down when an obstacle presents itself, brought his cousin, Tanaka Juri, along to help him deal with Taiga.
