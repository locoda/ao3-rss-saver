What is seen as dazzling doesn't mean that they're pretty inside. They have secrets and their own darkness.

"Sssshh," Hokuto took a deep breath, feeling his emotion deep through his lungs. He relaxed his chaotic mind, and told himself that everything is going to be okay.

With long legs and an outstanding appearance, Jesse made himself his own spotlight. Not to mention his good character, his capability to get into one’s heart makes everyone expect his presence.

As his photo session was done, Jesse went straight to his car. He was chased by the time, site to site to do recordings and stuff.

The admiration he got when he looked at showbiz was a big turning point in his life. All that always goes smoothly as he wishes is all vice versa. You? An actor? Did you lose your mind? You don’t even pretend to get some friends. How can you be an actor?

However, he needs to let his emotions out.

Hokuto is never satisfied with himself. Along with the ambition that finally took him to the spotlight, his self-doubt was reversely getting deeper along with the ridiculous idealism he had of himself. Hokuto can never be at ease. Every little thing that got in his mind always led him to countless sleepless nights, thinking about how he could keep going in this world without being useless and to be expected. If that's the case, why don’t you just lift it up by getting drunk anyway?
