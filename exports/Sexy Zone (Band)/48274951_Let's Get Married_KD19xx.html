Let's Get Married

It was Monday night and Fuma was just so exhausted, he just wanted to go home and lie down tomorrow is his birthday but he still have to work. He misses someone so badly and he hasn’t spent quality time with him because of their jampacked work schedules. Fuma is filming for two dramas, their promotion for their new single then they also have to prepare for their concert so he also needs to attend meetings and check with the equipment that needs to be used for the concert. He really wanted to spend his birthday with the man he loves but because of their schedules, they can’t do it, while he is inside their company car, he just looked out the window reminiscing about that time when they finally talked about their feelings for each other 9 years ago.

So he agreed to Yamapi’s suggestion to come with him on the last day of Kenty’s solo live. They entered the hall, gladly no one noticed him he is really hiding his face well much like he is hiding behind his senpai Yamapi. So even if he is really not prepared he is thankful for his Senpai after they talked about it because Fuma doesn’t want to part from Kenty and he finally gets to love the group they are in now.

He slowly opens the white curtains and there stands the man he was just thinking about a few minutes ago, he is wearing a white polo and black pants his face is illuminated and he looks so beautiful under the moonlight. Fuma didn’t realize that he has been standing there for minutes already, it felt like time stopped when he saw the man he loves all these years smiling at him.

Fuma went back to his senses and realized that he has been standing there for far too long “Oh sorry, you scared me. I thought someone forced themselves into my apartment like a burglar or something” he said.
