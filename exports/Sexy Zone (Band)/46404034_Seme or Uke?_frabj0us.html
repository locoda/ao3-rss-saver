Seme or Uke?

"So~ what are you?" Kento beamed at Fuma who was raising his eyebrows in response.

"What am I? What do you mean?"

Kento tsked, "You know, in the press conference you did today, the MC asked you about 'seme' and 'uke'."

Fuma nodded, "Then?"

Kento smiled widely, "What is your answer then? Since you didn't answer in the press conference, I was curious about it."

"What are you curious about? You know it already." Fuma chuckled and ruffled Kento's hair as he passed him towards the kitchen.

Kento frowned and followed Fuma, "What do you mean I know it? I don't know unless you tell me, Fuma!"

Fuma took a glass of water, drank it to the bottom and looked at Kento. "Come here, Kento."

Kento walked closer to Fuma and to his surprise, Fuma yanked him to his embrace. Fuma's hands circled around his waist and his face buried on his hair.

"I love your scent. What's your shampoo?" Fuma asked as he breathed in Kento's hair.

Kento put his hands on Fuma's chest, clutching on his shirt. "It's the same as yours, dummy."

Fuma humming approvingly, "But it smells different on you. It feels like home. I'm home."

Kento laughed, "Welcome home, Fuma."

Then, they went silent for a few minutes. They were enjoying each other's embrace and presence. It does feel like home, Kento thought. 

"Do you really want to know what my answer is?"

Kento, who had almost forgotten the conversation they were doing before, nodded and let Fuma continue.

"It's both."

"Eh?" Kento looked up at Fuma, his big brown eyes met Fuma's soft gaze.

"Either it's giving or receiving, I will get flustered, as long as it's you." Fuma leaned down and pecked Kento's nose tip.

Kento blinked and his cheeks flushed as he realised what Fuma meant. "I thought I was the one who was responsible for cheesy and romantic things?" He looked away and tried to get rid of the hot blush that creeped on his cheeks and ears.

Fuma chuckled, "Yes, you are." This time he aimed at Kento's plump lips and kissed him slowly.

"You know?" Fuma asked as he leaned back to took a breath. Kento, who was still breathing heavily and felt dizzy after the kiss, only shook his head.

Fuma tightened his embrace on Kento's waist, "I like both being undressed by you or being the one who is undressing you. But, do you know what I like the most?"

Kento shook his head again, "What is it?" He looked at Fuma and waited for his answer.

Fuma grinned widely, "What I like the most is when you're beneath me and I was inside you."

Kento's eyes widened and his cheeks flushed again, much redder than the previous.

"KIKUCHI FUMA!!!"

Fuma laughed, so loud and sounded annoying in Kento's ears. But, a few seconds later, Kento found himself laughing with Fuma.

It feels like home, no, it is home.
